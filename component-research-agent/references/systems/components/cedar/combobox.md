# Combobox

## Not Present
REI Cedar does not appear to include a dedicated Combobox component. REI's product filtering is handled through Cedar's Filter Chip and faceted search patterns rather than a combobox dropdown. For general filterable selection needs, Cedar's CdrSelect (native select) is used for shorter lists. Complex filterable dropdowns are not a prominent pattern in REI's retail-focused UI. Verify in Cedar's current documentation for any newer additions.

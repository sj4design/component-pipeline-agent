# Progress Indicator

## Approach
GOV.UK does not include a visual progress bar component. For multi-step form journeys, GOV.UK has moved away from progress indicators based on research showing they often mislead users (steps change as more info is gathered, users feel frustrated when estimated progress doesn't match reality). Instead, GOV.UK focuses on clear page headings and section navigation. The "Check Your Answers" page signals completion.

## Key Decisions
1. **No progress bar** (HIGH) — GOV.UK's user research found progress bars in multi-step forms often create anxiety and confusion. The current guidance discourages them for most service journeys.
2. **Section-based navigation** (MEDIUM) — For very long services, GOV.UK recommends a task list pattern that shows sections as complete/incomplete/in-progress states rather than a linear progress bar.

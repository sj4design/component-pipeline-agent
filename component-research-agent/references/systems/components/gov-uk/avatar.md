# Avatar

## Approach
GOV.UK Design System does not include an Avatar component. Government services typically identify users through text (name, email) or account reference numbers rather than profile images. The design system's focus on legibility and progressive enhancement makes image-based user identification out of scope for public sector services.

## Key Decisions
1. **No avatar component** (HIGH) — Government services do not use social-style avatar/profile photo patterns. User identification relies on text-based account references or names displayed in the service header.

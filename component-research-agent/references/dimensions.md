# 12 Standardized Dimensions

Every benchmark/research analyzes these dimensions for consistency and comparability.

## 1. Variants / Types
Types of the component (filled, outlined, ghost, etc.)

## 2. Sizes
Scale options (sm, md, lg, dense, compact)

## 3. Interactive States
Default, hover, focus, active, disabled, loading, error, selected

## 4. Anatomy
Constituent parts: container, label, icon, helper text, slots

## 5. Keyboard Navigation
Tab, Arrow keys, Enter, Escape, Home/End behaviors

## 6. ARIA & Roles
Roles, aria-labels, aria-expanded, live regions

## 7. Responsive Behavior
Mobile adaptation, breakpoints, touch targets

## 8. Motion / Animation
Transitions, duration, easing, prefers-reduced-motion

## 9. Theming & Tokens
Tokens used, customization, dark mode, brand override

## 10. Content Guidelines
Copy, truncation, i18n, min/max characters

## 11. Edge Cases
Overflow, empty states, async loading, data extremes

## 12. Dependencies
Child/parent components, composition, slots

## Scoring
For each dimension per system:
- ✅ Documented and complete
- ⚠️ Partially documented
- ❌ Not covered or not applicable
